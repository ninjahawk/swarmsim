E4: OFC avalanche size-duration relation E(T) (Exercise 5)
  E = total topplings, T = relaxation sweeps; fit E ~ T^gamma.

  alpha=0.15 : maxT=57  maxE=517  E ~ T^1.47
  alpha=0.20 : maxT=317  maxE=7594  E ~ T^1.64
  alpha=0.25 : maxT=1642  maxE=406996  E ~ T^1.89

  A power-law E ~ T^gamma with gamma > 1 reflects a compact spreading front:
  the toppled area accumulates faster than the avalanche's lifetime.
