     fixation -- even at the highest predation ~1/3 of the flock rides the shared escape as protected
     free-riders. The direction matches the prediction (intense predation erodes free-riding) but the
     magnitude is small: escape persists as a sticky mixed strategy, never collapsing and never fixing.
  -> Convergence: f=0.3 and f=0.7 starts settle into a similar band (0.67, 0.77); escape robustly
     persists as a substantial-but-not-fixed majority from either side, with some residual start-
     dependence and 2-seed noise -- a robust mixed strategy rather than one sharp fixed point.
