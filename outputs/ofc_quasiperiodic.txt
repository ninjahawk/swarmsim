  --> figures/ofc_quasiperiodic.png

  The nonconservative runs show a clear recurrence period that shrinks with
  alpha and vanishes at the conservative value 0.25 -- driven by spatial
  domains of locked nodal values (figure ofc_domains) that collapse and
  rebuild near-periodically.  This temporal structure is absent in the
  chapter-5 slope sandpile, whose avalanches are uncorrelated in time.
